Cleveland Browns vs. Tennessee Titans -2.5

GET THE LIVE NFL BETTING LINES NOW!   |  Get the Edge!

Cleveland is back in action after the initial bye week of the season against a team that looks like it's been on a bye for the last couple weeks there, in Tennessee. Although to be fair Tennessee did have that one shining moment where they went out on the road and just kicked Kansas City to the curb. That seems so long ago now its almost as if that never happened, (and the way Kansas Clobbered New England Monday night that just boggles the mind even more) yet it did and judging by where the betting action is going it looks like there might be some folks counting on seeing something like that happen once again. Yet, I must admit that over the last couple weeks Tennessee (along with Jacksonville, of course) have been sitting at the very top of our list. This week it's going to be a little trickier with Cleveland coming in there, as Cleveland has more than once made a case for consideration on our list, but as it looks now Cleveland has some modicum of mediorcity. They did after all, beat New Orleans, and covered (or heck were pretty close, especially if you got a favorable spread) in every game this season. That being said, we might have to consider that they have been playing somewhat over their heads, especially with such a small sample size. Tigers rarely change their stripes, and as far as Cleveland is concerned they are still... Cleveland. We have to assume that over time they will revert to the kind of performance that gets them onto our list. That statistical anomalies do normalize with a larger sample size. There is plenty of empirical data to suggest that will happen this week. Tennessee has beaten Cleveland in 7 of the last 10 meetings. As impressive as the win over New Orleans may have seen it was the only win in the last 10 games for Cleveland, and on the road they have lost the last 7 straight. In the end, you are what your record says you are, and yes as you extend the sample size the data will normalize. So taking all that into consideration this one has the makings of not just a loss, but an epic loss, the kind usually reserved for Thursday night. That being said the best way to ensure that happens if for me to pick against it.
