Sure San Diego is at home, but they were 0-2 at home in the preseason last year en route to going 1-3 under first year head coach Mike McCoy. Easily flouting one of our time tested tracking trends. Then there is the Dallas defense, there is just simply not any. So you can kind of see why I don't really like this one either. Now all that being said, I really don't see very much in the terms of depth offensively for San Diego, maybe not even enough from the front liners to get anything going against the very exploitable Dallas defense. So I can't really get behind an OVER bet when we are talking about 35 points here. Although that is probably just about as good as taking a side here in this one. Dallas does have a very impressive 6-2 ATS mark as a preseason underdog, and if I can get a whole FG in this one... even that very leaky Dallas defense can probably hold some water, I reckon.
